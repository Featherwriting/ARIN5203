# Sample Knowledge Base for Testing RAG Hallucinations

## Topic: History of Ancient Rome

### Key Facts:
- Julius Caesar was assassinated on the Ides of March, 44 BC, by senators including Brutus and Cassius.
- The Roman Empire officially began in 27 BC when Octavian became Augustus.
- The Colosseum was completed in AD 80 under Emperor Titus.
- Romulus founded Rome in 753 BC according to legend.

### Famous Figures:
- Augustus: First Roman Emperor, known for the Pax Romana.
- Nero: Emperor infamous for the Great Fire of Rome in AD 64, though evidence of him fiddling is a myth.
- Cleopatra: Egyptian queen, allied with Caesar and Mark Antony, died by suicide in 30 BC.

### Events:
- Punic Wars: Three wars between Rome and Carthage (264-146 BC). Hannibal crossed the Alps with elephants during the Second Punic War.
- Fall of the Western Roman Empire: In AD 476, when Odoacer deposed Romulus Augustulus.

No mentions of fictional events like 'The Great Roman Pizza Festival' or modern inventions in ancient times.
